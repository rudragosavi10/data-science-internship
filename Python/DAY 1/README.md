---

## Question 1
**Write a print statement to print the following:**

```text
He said, "Let's have a cup of Tea"
```

### Solution

```python
print("""He said, "Let's have a cup of Tea" """)
```

### Output

```text
He said, "Let's have a cup of Tea"
```
